I have been living in Toulouse for 5 years, from 2017 to 2022. In the first year I was focused on climbing the career ladder and that's how I found a Ph.D. position. Towards the end of my doctorate, I was ready to cover a relevant engineer role in a large airplane manufacturer. However, I was not chosen. Many other companies insisted to have me on board with them, so I cannot say to have felt unqualified.

My life was kind of stuck in a one-way road of getting an always better job, buying a house and contributing to the classical social rule world. A comfortable choice, where you don't worry about economical issues or healthcare, and where you can enjoy the little pleasures of life. I respect people who made this life choice, who are able to enjoy the comfort.
But everyone has its own addiction, and I think mine is getting out of my comfort zone. Setting new and harder goals to achieve, never stopping at the new levels I reached.
