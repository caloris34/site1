Don't just travel, Live the world

You all know about the 7 wonders, the thousands of blue beaches to make envious your colleagues at the office, or the "10 best things to do ..."

Many still find this type of travelling perfectly satisfying their expectations. But few souls out there, do not. I feel you. I am also a hidden gems hunter, always looking for living the most authentic and original experiences the world can offer to us. But finding these experiences can be very time consuming or even impossible for many.

That is why I decided to share my adventures here, tell the details of unique experiences but also provide full assistance in case you wish to organize such trips, from the cheap flight research to the authentic plans organization. Have a look at it here!

There is nothing natural or obvious about taking a holiday abroad.


A chimpanzee alpha male would never think of using his power in order to go on holiday into the territory of a neighbouring chimpanzee band. Yet, today we feel that in order to make the most of our human potential we must have as many different experiences as we can. We must open ourselves to a wide spectrum of emotions; we must sample various kinds of relationships; we must try different cuisines; we must learn to appreciate different styles of music. One of the best ways to do all that is to break free from our daily routine, leave behind our familiar setting, and go travelling in distant lands, where we can 'experience' the culture, the smells, the tastes and the norms of other people.

Sapiens - Y.N. Harari
