Ciao!
 
I'm Loris, an Italian descendent of Cristoforo Colombo, on a quest to shop some Indian spices across the sea and accidentally discover new worlds! After working 5 years locked in the office routine, I decided to become one of the first digital nomad aerospace engineer.
 
While traveling to about 30 countries in my 20s, I realized the pleasure of living a place rather than just visiting it. Know the people, join their culture, live their lives. Now, a citizen of the world, without a true home nor the desire to settle anywhere (yet). If you want to travel with me, give me or ask for an advice, or just say hi, send me a message here!
 
I hope to see you somewhere in this world. Don't forget to respect it and mostly to enjoy. Life is too short to not spend it doing and fighting for what we truly like. Everything else is ephemeral.
