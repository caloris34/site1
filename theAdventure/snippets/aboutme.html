Ciao! Thank you for visiting my blog!
 
My name is Loris, I am an Italian descendent of Cristoforo Colombo on my quest to grocery shop across the sea and accidentally discover new worlds! Even though originally from Ravenna on the Adriatic coast, I can say to belong to the Dolomites mountains, one of the most beautiful places on planet Earth. After working 5 years as aerospace engineer, I decided to leave everything behind and head towards the unknown. While visiting apprx 30 countries in my early 20s, I realized the pleasure of living a country rather than just visiting it.
 
That is the main objective of the voyage I find myself in. I intend to visit few corners in the world, stop and wander. Understand the culture, live the customs and traditions, meet the best people and helping in their projects. If you want to know more about the genesis of this project, you can give a read to this article in my blog.
 
If you want to travel with me, give me or ask for an advice, or just say hi, click the Whatsapp icon !
I hope to see you somewhere in this world. Don't forget to respect it and mostly to enjoy. Life is too short to not spend it doing what we like.
