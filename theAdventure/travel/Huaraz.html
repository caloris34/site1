Huaraz, the 2nd world's highest mountain range

Peru is not just Machu Picchu, lamas and colorful markets. Get ready to embark in one of the most challenging yet accessible hiking adventure planet Earth can offer you: the Cordillera Blanca.

Discover this mountain heaven with one of (or both!) these adventure: The Blanco Trek and The Blanco Expedition.

The Blanco Trek A multiple days trek of medium difficulty, with considerable height difference and kilometers every day. Crazy landscapes guaranteed.
The Blanco Expedition Summit some of the best glaciers in Peru above 5000m of altitude while learning mountain techniques. Not for faint hearted.

History

Most backpackers, busy in their hectic world discover, never heard of Huaraz. But take any Peruvian person and they would tell you it is a magical place. North of Lima along the Andes mountains lies the Cordillera Blanca, house of the highest peaks in Peru up to 6800m. At its center, Huaraz is the Peruvian hiking Mecca, from which you can see snow capped mountain around you all year long. At 3000 meters altitude in the Ancash region, a night bus away north from Lima, Huaraz and its 150.000 people live in the Cordillera Blanca. Mountains are everywhere around you, in any direction you will look at, and with most probability you will see Huascaran, the highest mountain of Peru at 6800 meters.
